The AFEOL Project is built on principles of privacy, anonymity, cryptographic integrity, and respectful collaboration.  
All contributors, maintainers, and community members must follow the rules defined below.

---

## 1. Core Principles

- Respect privacy and anonymity of all contributors  
- No harassment, discrimination, or intimidation  
- No logging, tracking, recording, or collecting contributor metadata  
- Keep discussions technical, professional, and solution-oriented  
- Protect the security and integrity of AFEOL standards  

AFEOL is a **privacy-centric**, **defensive**, and **research-oriented** project.  
All behavior must align with these values.

---

## 2. Expected Behavior

All participants must:

- Communicate respectfully and avoid personal attacks  
- Provide constructive, relevant feedback  
- Follow the AFEOL Coding Standard and Documentation Standard  
- Protect sensitive information shared through Tor/I2P channels  
- Prioritize security, anonymity, and cryptographic correctness  
- Help maintain a positive, research-friendly environment  

---

## 3. Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are strictly forbidden:

- Harassment, disrespect, insults, or aggressive communication  
- Releasing, storing, or exposing personal data of contributors  
- Doxxing or attempts to identify anonymous contributors  
- Discriminatory remarks (race, gender, ethnicity, beliefs, etc.)  
- Advocating illegal activity  
- Introducing malicious, harmful, or insecure code  
- Sneaking telemetry, trackers, analytics, cloud tracking, or metadata leaks  
- Attempting to weaken anonymity, encryption, or network safety  

Any contributor engaging in such actions will be permanently removed from the project.

## 5. Enforcement Guidelines

Maintainers may take the following actions:

### 1. Warning  
For minor, first-time infractions. Delivered privately.

### 2. Temporary Restriction  
Contributor blocked from activity for a limited period.

### 3. Permanent Removal  
For repeat or severe violations (harassment, malicious intent, security risk).

### 4. Rejection of Contributions  
Any PR or issue containing harmful or prohibited content will be rejected immediately.

All enforcement decisions are final.

---

## 6. AFEOL Security & Ethics Clause

All contributors acknowledge that:

- AFEOL exists to protect privacy, encryption, and secure communication  
- Contributions must **not** support or encourage illegal behavior  
- All changes must respect Tor/I2P threat models  
- Research must remain defensive in nature  
- Responsible disclosure procedures apply  

---

## 7. Commitment to the Community

AFEOL is a collective, decentralized effort.  
Our strength comes from professionalism, cryptographic discipline, and mutual respect.  
By participating, you affirm your commitment to these principles.
